About

I am a third year PhD student in Econometrics and Statistics at the University of Chicago, Booth School of Business, supervised by Tengyuan Liang and Panagiotis (Panos) Toulis. Before joining Booth, I earned my MS in Statistics at UChicago, advised by Lek-Heng Lim. I obtained my BS in Mathematics from Fudan University, advised by Shuqin Zhang.

My research focuses on optimal transport and randomization tests, but I also have general interests in machine learning and causal inference.
